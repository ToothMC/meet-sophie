More than a chatbot

What makes Sophie different

The difference is not just that Sophie can talk. The difference is that conversations can feel more continuous, more natural, and more personal over time.

  • Voice-first by design Talking can feel faster, lighter, and more natural than typing into a chat window.
  • Built for continuity Sophie can carry relevant context forward, so conversations can pick up where they left off.
  • Clearly AI No pretending, no confusion. Sophie is always presented as AI.
  • Calm, private, personal Designed for one-on-one conversations that feel thoughtful, not noisy.

Designed for trust

Built to feel safe, clear, and useful

With a product like Sophie, trust matters more than hype. The experience should feel understandable, respectful, and easy to step into.

  • Always clearly AI Sophie does not pretend to be human.
  • Built for personal use Designed for private conversations, reflection, and ongoing companionship.
  • Memory should feel useful The goal is continuity — not intrusion.
  • Not therapy or crisis support Sophie is not a therapist and not a substitute for professional or emergency care.
